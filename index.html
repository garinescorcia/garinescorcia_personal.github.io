Who I am ?

A career as Mechatronic engineer and Researcher I had the opportunity to work as an assistant in the maintenance area of a factory. In addition, the opportunity to work on the research field as Ph. I am also capable of designing software programs to control these tools. Moreover, I participate actively and lead technology presentations at scientific and industrial events.
